Step-by-Step: How to Use Split Bill Buddy

1.Register or Login

Create your account or login to access your groups, track expenses, and view settlements across devices. Your data stays synced and secure.

2.Create a Group

First, create a new group by entering group name and member names. Once you create a group, it will appear under “Your Groups”. This group becomes your shared space where you and your members can start tracking expenses together.

3.Add expenses

Once your group is set up, start adding shared expenses. Just enter what the expense was for, how much was paid, and who paid it. You can also choose how to split it, add notes, pick a date, and upload a receipt.
